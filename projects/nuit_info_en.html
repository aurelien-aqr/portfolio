Nuit de l'Info 2022 (National Hackathon)

  • Skills used: Teamwork, Web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), Video game development (Python, Ren’Py)
  • Links: Live website | MSTCity game

Nuit de l’Info is a national French hackathon that brings together students, teachers, and companies to collaborate on the development of a web application within a single night. During this event, I took part in selecting the challenges to tackle, helped lead the design of the video game we created, and took initiative throughout the competition.

The main theme that year was sexual health awareness. We were asked to design an interactive web application aimed at educating teenagers and adults about sexual health. Our team created a website featuring several mini-games focused on sexually transmitted infections and their risks. In particular, we developed a Ren’Py visual novel called MSTCity, a dating-simulation-style game in which the player must make the right choices to avoid contracting an STI.

We achieved 3rd place nationally out of 518 participants.

Within a team of seven people, I developed most of the MSTCity game.
