About Me

My name is Aurélien Alquier, and I have been passionate about computer science for as long as I can remember. This passion led me to follow the academic and professional path presented in this portfolio.

I am currently an engineering student at IMT Mines Alès, majoring in Computer Science and Networks, in a work-study software development program (2025-2028). Previously, I earned a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science (2022-2025) at IUT Montpellier-Sète, RACDV track (Application Development: Design, Development, Validation).

I am proficient in a variety of programming languages: Java, Kotlin, Python, PHP, HTML/CSS, SQL, Bash, and C. I have solid experience in Android development (Java/Kotlin/Compose), and foundational skills in JavaScript and JavaFX.

My desire to explore the broader field of computing also led me to join INSA Lyon for a one-year introductory engineering program. This experience expanded my knowledge in engineering sciences and helps me approach technical problems with a more global perspective.

I applied my skills during a 2024 internship at Lundi Matin as a Python web developer, working on the backend of IZBERG, an API-first marketplace platform.

In 2024-2025, I continued at Lundi Matin in a work-study position as an Android mobile developer, working on a POS software solution (Java/XML and Kotlin/Compose).

Since 2025, I have been a Data/AI engineering apprentice in the IT department of France Travail (Castelnau-le-Lez), at the intersection of data engineering and AIOps (Python data pipelines, CI/CD, observability/monitoring).

Beyond computer science, I enjoy exploring new cultures and languages. I am fluent in both French and English, have some knowledge of Spanish, and I am currently learning Japanese, a language and culture I am especially passionate about.

I am continuing my engineering studies to specialize in data/AI-oriented software development. My goal is to become a software engineer by combining software engineering, data engineering/MLOps, and project management.
